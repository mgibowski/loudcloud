Loudcloud is free and open-source. It was made for fun and education.

Loudcloud is an open-source project that you are very welcome to fork on github.

The idea came about in conversations between Jacob and Michał in the old days when there was no Spotify in Germany and SoundCloud didn't allow users to create playlists.

First version was implemented by Jacob with Node.js and the one you are just looking at by Michał with Scala, Playframework 2 and some Akka actors. This time the motivation was to present these technologies at the Devcrowd conference in Szczecin, Poland.
